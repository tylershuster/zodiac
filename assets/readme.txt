This interactive web page shows you a zodiac chart for a given time and place.
Click the Earth to choose your location.
Click the time text to choose the time.
Astrological symbols represent planets.
The four elements in the corners show which planets lie in signs associated with those elements.
The trails behind planets show the direction they will move at that point in time, to determine retrogrades.
Ruling planets have a purple glow, exalted planets yellow. Deteriorated planets and fallen planets appear in two degrees of distortion.
The houses of the sun appear on the outside of the chart.
